Thai Tea Shortbread Cookies

Description

These soft and buttery shortbread cookies are embedded with a mix for Thai iced tea, and covereding with a thai iced tea icing.

Ingredients

  • Cookies
    • 1 cup butter
    • 0.5 cup powdered sugar
    • 2 cup all purpose flour
    • 0.5 tsp salt
    • 6 tsp spoons loose Thai tea mix
  • Icing
    • 1 cup powdered sugar
    • 2 tbsp concentrated Thai tea
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Peheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream butter and confectioners sugar in a large bowl or stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
  3. Add in flour, salt, and loose tea leaves. Continue to mix until a soft dough forms, the mix will be dry and crumbly at first but will come together.
  4. Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface until it’s ¼ inch thick.
  5. Cut out cookies using a 2 in round cookie cutter.
  6. Bake on a parchment-lined baking sheet for 18-20 minutes or until edges are a golden brown and transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely.
  7. Once cooled, whisk together icing ingredients until smooth. Dip half of each cookie in the icing, let excess drip off and run a spoon along the edge to remove any extra, then place on wax paper to set.
